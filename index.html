My favourite wines

Red Wines

Mauro
This is a red wine belonging to Ribera del Duero PDO. We have tested year 2022 and right now it is one of the my favs. Very well balanced, ideal to drink with some red meat. Good value for money.
Mil cepas
This one is a bit more unsual with a Bobal grape, typical from Utiel-Requena area (Valencia PDO). It belongs to Bodega Manzanique. Price is around 25 EUR.

White Wines

Viña Tondonia Reserva Blanco
This one is an expensive and exclusive white wine. I have tested years 2008 and 2011. The best white wine I have ever tested, perfectly balance, belonging to Rioja PDO and to López de Heredia cellar.
Heredad Altos de Talana
Bierzo PDO; Godello variety. Price around 24 EUR per bottle.
